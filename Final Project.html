The Sinister Source of Our Conservation Funding

Final Project: Story Telling With Data

Peter W. Schoffelen

Intro

<

Despite declining numbers in hunters, the excise tax appropriations from Robert-Pittman rose substantially during the Obama administration(most likely explained by gun sales driven by fear of stricter gun laws), and that the decline (less fearful of stricter gun laws) during the Trump administration makes aspect like a Backpack Tax (a tax on equipment sold from REI) possibly good solutions to sustaining conservation funding while also advancing more progressive gun control.
